Frequently Asked Questions

Q1) What is the admission procedure and what are the eligibility criteria to get admission?

Candidate may get admission through CAP or Management Quota. 80% admissions are available for CAP and 20% will be made at Institute Level.

For detailed information, please visit: DTE Maharashtra.

Only CET or JEE Main Paper 1 candidates can apply for CAP for first-year admission.

Candidate must have 50% in PCM (open category) and 45% in PCM (in case of other backward categories).

Note: Physics and Mathematics are compulsory subjects, and Chemistry can be substituted with any technical or vocational subject.

Detailed information about institute level admission can be obtained from the admission cell of the college. We request you to visit our college on any working day between 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Note: Online registration is compulsory for management quota seats. Detailed information (fees related) will be obtained from the college admission center. (contact: 18005322525)

Q2) What about the training and placement activities?

We have a proactive Training and Placement Cell. We provide skill-based training and placement assistance to all our final-year students. We have a good track record of placement.

Our major recruiters on campus are TCS, Tech Mahindra, INFOSYS, TATA HONEYWELL, TATA, L&T, INFOTECH, COGNIZANT TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS, NEWGEN, GODREJ, CALSOFT, CAPGEMINI, and many more.

Visit the link for the detailed list: JITNashik Recruiters.

Institute also provides in-house training for soft skills, communication skills, aptitude, group discussions, personal interviews, and add-on technical courses.

Q3) What is the fee structure?

For the fee structure for the first-year engineering, please refer to the admission menu.

Every year, the fee gets revised according to the Fees Regulatory Authority.

Q4) Do you have transport facilities?

Yes, we have our own transport facility.

Q5) What is the branch-wise intake capacity?

Intake for various branches is as follows:

  • Under Graduate Level (First-year Engineering)
    • Computer Engineering
    • Information Technology
    • Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
    • Mechanical Engineering
    • Civil Engineering
    • Electrical Engineering
    • Electronics & Computer Engineering
  • Under Graduate Level (Direct Second-year Engineering)
    • Computer Engineering: As per the available vacancies
    • Mechanical Engineering: As per the available vacancies
    • Civil Engineering: As per the available vacancies
    • Electrical Engineering: As per the available vacancies
    • Information Technology: As per the available vacancies
    • Artificial Intelligence and Data Science: As per the available vacancies

Q6) What is the percentage of scholarship applicable for reserved category students?

SC-100%, ST-100%, EBC/OBC-50%, VJNT-100%: Scholarship on tuition fee.

Q7) What are the other facilities available?

We provide book bank facility, library facility (8:00 AM to 6:00 PM), and WiFi facility for students. The institute also has a Gymkhana and a sports ground for the students to develop their extracurricular skills.

Full fee reimbursement for university toppers.

Fee concession for good scholars according to their marks.

Fee concession for EBC/TFWS students as per DTE rules.

Q8) Do you provide hostel facilities?

Yes, we provide hostel facilities.

Q9) What is the fee structure for hostel facilities?

Approximately, hostel charges will be Rs. 45,000/- per year.

Q10) What are the working hours of the institute?

Working hours of the institute are Mon to Fri: 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM and on ODD Saturdays: 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM.
