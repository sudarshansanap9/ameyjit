Message from the Head of Applied Science and Humanities Department

Welcome to the Applied Science and Humanities Department at JIT Nashik! Our department is dedicated to providing a comprehensive education that integrates the essential principles of both the sciences and the humanities. This interdisciplinary approach equips students with a well-rounded skill set, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and ethical awareness.

We pride ourselves on our innovative teaching methodologies, state-of-the-art facilities, and a passionate faculty team that brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to the classroom. Our students benefit from diverse research opportunities, interdisciplinary projects, and community engagement activities, all of which enrich their educational journey.

As the Head of the Department, I am committed to promoting academic excellence, pioneering interdisciplinary research, and fostering a culture of inclusivity and lifelong learning. I invite you to explore our programs and join us in our mission to advance knowledge and societal well-being through applied science and humanities.

Sincerely,

Prof. Dr. A. K. Sanap
Head of Applied Science and Humanities Department, JIT Nashik
