Welcome to AITS - Association of Information Technology Students

Overview

AITS (Association of Information Technology Students) is a collaboration of all the IT students and the staff of the college. It unites all the students to participate and contribute towards the activities organized by all year students of the Information Technology field. IT is integral to numerous aspects of our modern world. AITS of JIT, Nashik offers programs that consistently strive to provide a support system and enrich knowledge for students in the field of information technology. This unique program is established to provide students with not only basic IT skills but also enriching experiences to shape their future as well. AITS of JIT, Nashik was founded with a vision to explore beyond the boundaries of textbooks and thus invent a whole new world of innovations for IT professionals. AITS organizes various programs such as seminars, webinars, alumni meets, online projects, and placement training with techfest being one of its most successful endeavors.

Goals

AITS aims at developing the essential tasks involving IT professionals which include designing, analyzing, and implementing information systems. AITS prepares students to meet the ever-increasing global challenges of rapidly evolving technologies to prepare the students for both industries and higher institutions of learning.

Scope

AITS strives to advance the theory, practice, and application of information technology and maintains a high professional standard among its members. It promotes cooperation and the exchange of technical information among its members by conducting various seminars and workshops.

Activities

  • National Hackathon - 200 participants
  • Teachers Day Celebration by organizing a Coding Workshop for the local community.
  • On the occasion of Engineers Day, the Association organized an IT Expo with presentations and video clips showcasing various projects.
